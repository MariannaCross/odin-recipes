Mother's Meatloaf

Meatloaf can be so soft and comforting,

a meal made by Mother, just for us.

Is it bread? What deceit! It's only shaped

like bread, hence a loaf, of meat.

Mother doesn't play with the meat

as she shapes it carefully, with her hands.

It smells raw, before it's cooked.

The smell is familiar, for sure.

Could we make a meatloaf statue?

Ingredients

  • ambiguous meat
  • spices
  • herbs
  • ketchup
  • eggs
  • bread crumbles
  • something of Mother

Steps

  1. grind the meat into an undefinable mass
  2. mix the meat with your fingers
  3. keep the eggshells for easter trinkets
  4. sprinkle bread crumbles wherever you go
  5. never get lost
  6. add something of Mother, was it her hair?
  7. bake
