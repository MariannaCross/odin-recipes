Pasta Taco

I'll admit, it's not what I thought.

A pasta taco? Surely, that should be spaghetti

inside a taco shell, alas ...

Someone has boiled pasta of varying lengths

and wrapped it around some taco meat

Is there more pasta on top?

Or could that be cheese?

Some people fix foods with cheese.

There's no fixing this monstrosity.

Ingredients

  • ground meat
  • taco seasoning
  • pasta
  • cheese
  • a lime

Steps

  1. boil all the pasta
  2. fry the meat until it sizzles
  3. season the meat with "taco" seasoning
  4. add cheese, if you can.
  5. gift a lime to your friends, and apologize for your cultural crimes
  6. eat it all. Mother does not approve of waste
