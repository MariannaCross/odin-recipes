An Easter Rabbit

Rabbit bones are beautiful,

their legs, braised and placed on the plate,

stretch to God;

a slender limb elegant in silence,

longing for Him to impart the spark of life,

fervent prayers for more verdant fields,

for more spring breezes,

for alert ears and perked noses giving glory to the sun.

How gruesome, to be so deliciously dead.

Ingredients

  • rabbit leg
  • olive oil, 1/4 cup
  • large onion
  • garlic cloves, 4
  • chicken broth, 2 cups
  • thyme, to taste
  • lemon juice, 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. hold the rabbit meat
  2. observe the absence of life
  3. cook the flesh so it can't harm you
  4. consume
